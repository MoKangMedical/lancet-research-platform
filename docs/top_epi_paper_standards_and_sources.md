# Top Epidemiology Paper Standards and Sources

Last updated: 2026-03-06

## Core Reporting Standards
- STROBE (observational studies): https://www.equator-network.org/reporting-guidelines/strobe/
- RECORD (routinely collected health data): https://www.equator-network.org/reporting-guidelines/record/
- GATHER (global health estimates / burden modeling): https://www.equator-network.org/reporting-guidelines/gather-statement/
- TRIPOD (prediction model reporting): https://www.tripod-statement.org/

## Publishing and Ethics Standards
- ICMJE Recommendations: https://www.icmje.org/recommendations/
- COPE Guidelines: https://publicationethics.org/guidance/Guidelines
